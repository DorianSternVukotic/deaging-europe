summary: Androgenic alopecia and ageing-related miniaturisation respond best to evidence-tier 1 medications combined with mechanical stimulation.
solutions:
  - name: Topical minoxidil 5 %
    kind: ingredient
    description: First-line evidence-based regrowth treatment. Visible density at 4–6 months. Must be used continuously.
    evidence: strong
  - name: Oral finasteride / dutasteride
    kind: ingredient
    description: Blocks DHT, the hormone driving male-pattern loss. Requires medical supervision.
    evidence: strong
  - name: Microneedling 1.5 mm weekly
    kind: treatment
    description: Combined with minoxidil, RCTs show ~4× the regrowth vs minoxidil alone.
    evidence: strong
  - name: Low-level laser therapy (LLLT)
    kind: device
    description: Helmets and caps with red-light diodes (650 nm) modestly improve density over 6 months.
    evidence: moderate

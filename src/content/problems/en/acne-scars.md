    evidence: strong
  - name: TCA CROSS
    kind: treatment
    description: High-concentration trichloroacetic acid spot-applied to icepick scars. Cheap, dermatologist-only.
    evidence: strong
  - name: Subcision
    kind: treatment
    description: Manual or device-driven release of fibrotic tethers under rolling scars. Often combined with filler.
    evidence: strong
  - name: Tretinoin
    kind: ingredient
    description: Daily use prevents new scars and modestly remodels superficial texture between in-clinic sessions.
    evidence: strong
